04.Cookies

The Site uses cookies to provide a better and more relevant experience.

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small file that a website sends to your browser and stores on your device. It helps the site remember information about your visit.

How we use cookies

  • To understand how visitors use the Site
  • To provide more relevant content based on your activity
  • For advertising targeting and performance measurement

How to disable cookies

You can refuse or remove cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data
  • Safari: Settings → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Note: If you disable cookies, some features of the Site may not work properly.
